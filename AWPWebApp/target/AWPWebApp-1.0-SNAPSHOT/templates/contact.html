Directions and Map


New York City has three major airports: La Guardia Airport, Newark Liberty International Airport, and JFK International Airport. All major airlines provide service to all three. All major car rental firms operate at the airports; taxis and limousine service are also available. Transfers between the airport and the Hotel can be arranged through the Concierge for an additional fee.
